Hannah Gregory is a writer and editor. She has over a decade of experience working with words in cultural contexts, in London, Paris, Milan, and Berlin. Her writing on art, places, and relations has appeared in Afterall, Art Monthly, The New Inquiry, The White Review, The Wire, and more. Her editing focus is prose, in particular contemporary literature and arts writing. She has given readings at Bergen Kunsthall, Haus der Kulturen der Welt, Hayward Gallery, and Mudam, among others. From 2021–25 she held ‘ambient writing hour’, a space for writing in company online. She founded the Parens Editors group in 2020. <a href="https://hannahgregory.substack.com/about">Receive her letters here</a>.
